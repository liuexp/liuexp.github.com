Short Bio

I am a first second year PhD student in the theory group at UC Berkeley. My advisor is Alistair Sinclair.

Before coming to Berkeley, I did my undergraduate studies in Computer Science, in the ACM Honored Class 2010 at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
